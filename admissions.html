Admissions Process

We welcome applications from students who demonstrate academic potential and a commitment to our school values. Our admissions process is designed to be straightforward and transparent.

1

Inquiry

Contact our admissions office to learn more about our programs.

2

Application

Complete the application form and submit required documents.

3

Assessment

Students may be required to take entrance exams or participate in interviews.

4

Admission Decision

Receive notification of acceptance and enrollment details.

Admission Requirements

To be considered for admission, applicants must meet the following requirements:

New Students

  • Completed application form
  • Recent passport photograph
  • Previous school report

Transfer Students

  • All new student requirements
  • Transfer letter from previous school
  • Current term report card

International Students

  • All new student requirements
  • Valid student visa
  • Medical examination report
  • English proficiency test results (if applicable)
