Our History

Founded in 2018 by a group of visionary educators, Midland High School Mukono began as a small secondary school with just over 47 students and 5 teachers. Our founders believed in providing quality education that would develop both the mind and character of young Ugandans.

Over the years, the school has expanded with different facilities and programs.

Throughout our history, we have maintained our commitment to traditional values while embracing innovation in education. Our alumni network includes leaders in different institutions, business, academia, and the arts across Uganda and beyond.

Today, Midland High School Mukono serves over 1,200 students from diverse backgrounds, continuing our legacy of transforming lives through education.

Our Mission

To provide a transformative education that develops students intellectually, morally, and socially, preparing them to be responsible leaders and engaged citizens in a global society.

Our Vision

To be Uganda's premier educational institution recognized for academic excellence, character development, and innovative learning that empowers students to reach their full potential.
