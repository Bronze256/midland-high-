Academic Facilities

Our state-of-the-art academic facilities provide the perfect environment for effective teaching and learning.

Modern Classrooms

Spacious, well-lit classrooms equipped with smart boards, projectors, and ergonomic furniture to enhance the learning experience.

Resource Center

Our extensive library houses over 15,000 volumes, digital resources, quiet study areas, and computer stations for research.

Science Laboratories

Fully equipped laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology with modern equipment for practical experiments and research.

Computer Labs

Three modern computer labs with high-speed internet, latest software, and 1:1 student-computer ratio for ICT education.

Auditorium

500-seat multi-purpose auditorium with professional sound and lighting systems for assemblies, performances, and lectures.

Creative Arts Studio

Dedicated spaces for visual arts, music, and drama with professional equipment and materials for creative expression.

Sports & Recreation

We believe in holistic development through physical education and competitive sports.

Football Pitch

Full-size football field with professional turf and seating for 300 spectators.

Basketball Court

Two outdoor courts with floodlights for evening practice and competitions.

Volleyball Court

Professional standard sand court for beach volleyball training.

Athletics Track

400m synthetic track for sprinting, hurdles, and long-distance running.

Indoor Games

Table tennis, chess, and board games in our recreation center.

Swimming Pool

Olympic-size swimming pool with certified lifeguards and instructors.

Technology & Innovation

Preparing students for the digital future with cutting-edge technology resources.

STEM Lab

Dedicated space for robotics, coding, and engineering projects with 3D printers and prototyping tools.

Wi-Fi Campus

High-speed wireless internet coverage across the entire school for research and learning.

Digital Library

Access to thousands of e-books, academic journals, and online learning platforms.

Smart Classrooms

Interactive whiteboards and audio-visual equipment in all classrooms for enhanced learning.

Boarding Facilities

Safe, comfortable, and conducive residential facilities for our boarding students.

Dormitories

Spacious dormitories with bunk beds, personal storage, and common areas. Separate houses for boys and girls.

Dining Hall

Modern dining facility serving balanced, nutritious meals prepared by professional catering staff.

Health Center

Fully equipped medical facility with a full-time nurse and visiting doctor for student healthcare needs.

Counseling Center

Professional counseling services for academic, career, and personal development guidance.
