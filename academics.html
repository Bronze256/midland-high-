Our Academic Programs

Core Curriculum

All students take compulsory subjects including English, Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry, Physics, History, Geography, and Religious Education.

Elective Options

Students can choose additional subjects from Luganda, Literature in English, Computer Studies, Agriculture, and Commerce.

Special Features

Enhanced DIT program with different clubs, science fairs, and innovation competitions.

Science Combination

Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Mathematics, Subsidiary ICT/Mathematics. Prepares students for engineering, medicine, and technology fields.

Arts Combination

History, Economics, Geography/Literature, Subsidiary ICT/Mathematics. Ideal for law, business, and social sciences.

Special Programs

University preparation workshops, career counseling, and research projects with local universities.

Computer Science

Practical training in programming, web development, and computer maintenance with industry certifications.

Catering and bakery

Baking and preparation of different recipes.

Agricultural Science

Modern farming techniques, agribusiness management using our school farm.

Curriculum Overview

Our curriculum follows the Uganda National Curriculum but is enhanced with additional programs:

  • Uganda National Examination Board (UNEB) syllabus
  • DIT enrichment program
  • Leadership development curriculum
  • Digital literacy integration across all subjects
  • Entrepreneurship training
  • Environmental education
  • Career guidance program
  • Community service requirement
  • Creative arts program
